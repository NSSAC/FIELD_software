EPIDEMIOLOGICAL COMMENTS

This follow-up report includes updates to detections of highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) H5 clade 2.3.4.4b in poultry flocks in which N type was not determined by sequencing or additional N type reassortments were detected. The USDA Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS), in conjunction with State Animal Health and Wildlife Officials, are conducting comprehensive epidemiological investigations and enhanced surveillance in response to the HPAI related events.
